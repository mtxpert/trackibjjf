Help

How to track athletes

Pick a tournament → search your school or athlete name → select athletes to watch → tap Watch. Updates happen automatically every ~30 seconds.

Fight alerts (push notifications)

While watching athletes, tap Enable Alerts to get push notifications when a fight is scheduled or completed — even with the app closed. On iPhone, first tap the share button and Add to Home Screen, then enable alerts.

Install as an app

Android/Chrome: Tap the menu (⋮) → Add to Home Screen or Install App.
iPhone/Safari: Tap the share button (□↑) → Add to Home Screen.

Gym codes

Gym Pack and Affiliate Pack subscribers get access codes to share with coaches. Each code unlocks the Individual plan for one person. Manage your codes via Account → Manage Team Codes.

Free vs paid

Free accounts can track 1 athlete. Individual Coach ($2.99/mo) removes that limit. Gym and Affiliate packs cover whole teams.
